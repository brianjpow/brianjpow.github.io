My name is Brian Powers and I've been designing and developing games and interactive experiences for over 10 years. I'm passionate about seamless and elegant designs in all areas of life whether it's a mobile app, a desktop website, or even the way a room is laid out. Design influences every area of our life and it should always be clean, responsive, and easy to understand.

Over the course of my career I've shipped a diverse catalog of mobile and desktop products. On nearly all these projects I've had to wear multiple hats from design, prototyping, art, testing, marketing, and so much more. I'm almost entirely self taught and have constantly evolved in my understanding of what drives user engagement, retention and conversions. I'm always learning and striving to improve myself and anything I work on.
