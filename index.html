A little about myself
My name is Brian Powers and I've been designing and developing games and interactive experiences for over 10 years. I'm passionate about seamless and elegant designs in all areas of life whether it's a mobile app, a desktop website, or even the way a room is laid out. Design influences every area of our life and it should always be clean, responsive, and easy to understand.

Over the course of my career I've shipped a diverse catalog of mobile and desktop products. On nearly all these projects I've had to do a little bit of everything, design, prototyping, art, testing, marketing, and so much more. I've been almost entirely self taught and have constantly evolved in my understanding of what drives user engagement, retention and conversions. I'm always learning and striving to improve myself and anything I work on. Passion for life is what allows us to be successful
